A Little Bit About Us

The team abides by the Agile Manifesto and subscribes to the Scrum methodology. While the values on the right are valuable, we value the ones on the left more:

  1. Individuals and interactions over processes and tools
  2. Working software over comprehensive documentation
  3. Customer collaboration over contract negotiation
  4. Responding to change over following a plan

Sprints are 1 week long and daily standups are held everyday. The product manager is Jeremy Ronquillo, and Scrum Master is Nathan Verlin.

Most importantly, we believe that teamwork makes the dream work.
