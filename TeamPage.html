A Little Bit About Us

Team Cache Money was born in the early January of Spring 2017 in University of the Pacific's COMP129:Software Engineering class. The team is comprised of 6 members of diverse background. The project that CacheMoney is working on will be described in the section below. The team abides by the Agile Manifesto and subscribes to the Scrum methodology. While the values on the right are valuable, we value the ones on the left more:

  1. Individuals and interactions over processes and tools
  2. Working software over comprehensive documentation
  3. Customer collaboration over contract negotiation
  4. Responding to change over following a plan

Sprints are 1 week long and daily standups are held everyday. The product manager is Jeremy Ronquillo, and Scrum Master is Nathan Verlin.

Most importantly, we believe that teamwork makes the dream work.
